So, that's what we did. We built the FortisEDGE platform, powered by FortisGPT and Phil — our persistent memory, learning AI agent. A thorough analysis of our competitors confirms we are the first to combine all of these elements, benefiting both brokers and clients. What you're looking at is not a feature list. It is the result of two-plus decades of real-world frustration with tools that were never built for people who actually close commercial real estate transactions for a living.

When you join Fortis Capital Solutions as a full-time agent, you don't get access to a CRM with a few AI add-ons bolted on. You get FortisEDGE powered by FortisGPT — a proprietary intelligence platform with 68 tools embedded into every layer of how you market, prospect, match buyers, prepare for calls, manage deals, and close. Phil is not a chatbot. He carries the full context of our 177,000-contact verified investor database, understands deal nuance across six asset verticals, and runs continuously on your behalf — whether you're on a call or sleeping.

There is one thing we want to be direct about: FortisEDGE is not for sale. It is not a subscription. It is not available to outside brokers under any circumstances. It is an exclusive operational benefit for Fortis agents — and it is the reason our agents close faster, target more precisely, and operate with a level of intelligence that no competing firm can currently match.
